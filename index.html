Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams, each consisting of eleven players, on a circular or oval-shaped field. It originated in England and is widely popular in countries such as India, Australia, England, Pakistan, South Africa, and the West Indies, among others.
Cricket is not just a sport but also a cultural phenomenon in many countries, with matches often attracting large crowds and intense national rivalries. Its complexity, strategy, and the unique balance between individual performance and team dynamics make it a fascinating sport for both players and spectators.
